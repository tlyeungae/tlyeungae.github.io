Risk Vulnerability and Portfolio Choices over Life Cycle

Abstract: ▼

Focusing on the portfolio effects of risk vulnerability and using the SIPP, a comprehensive panel of U.S. households, this paper identifies that young workers' heightened vulnerability to labor income risk contributes to their hesitation in holding risky assets.
